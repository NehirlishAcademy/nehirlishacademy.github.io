Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal data Nehirlish Academy collects, why, and how it is handled. Your privacy matters and your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

Last updated: June 2026

01 · Who We Are

Data Controller

Nehirlish Academy is operated by Nehir Roza Kılıç, based in Istanbul, Turkey. This website is hosted on Netlify (netlify.com).

For any privacy-related questions or requests: Nehirlishh@gmail.com

02 · What We Collect

Personal Data

We collect only what is necessary. Here is exactly what that means for Nehirlish Academy:

  • Contact form: When you fill in the contact form, we collect your name, email address, the service you're interested in, and any message you choose to share. This is used only to respond to your enquiry.
  • Netlify hosting data: Netlify, our hosting provider, automatically collects basic technical data such as IP addresses and browser information as part of standard web hosting. This is governed by Netlify's Privacy Policy.
  • Lesson communication: If you become a student, information you share during lessons or via email (such as your learning goals) is used solely to deliver your lessons. It is never stored beyond what is necessary.
No payment data collected on this site

Payments are handled directly and personally · no payment details are collected or stored through this website.

03 · How We Use It

Purpose of Processing

  • To respond to enquiries submitted through the contact form.
  • To deliver 1-on-1 lessons, group classes, or course access you have arranged.
  • To communicate lesson schedules, resources, or updates relevant to your learning.

Your data is never used for advertising, sold to third parties, or shared with anyone outside of what is needed to deliver the service.

04 · Cookies

Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics tools. The only cookies that may be set are functional ones used by Netlify's infrastructure as part of standard web hosting.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party tracking service.

05 · Data Storage

Where Your Data Lives

Contact form submissions are sent directly to Nehirlish Academy via email and are not stored in a separate database. Netlify may temporarily process form data as part of form delivery · see Netlify's Privacy Policy for details.

We do not retain your personal data beyond what is needed for the purpose it was collected. If you were a student and our lessons have ended, your contact information is not kept on file unless you have an active course access arrangement.

06 · Your Rights

KVKK & Your Rights

Under Turkey's KVKK (Law No. 6698) and in line with GDPR principles, you have the right to:

  • Know whether your personal data is being processed.
  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to the processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact Nehirlishh@gmail.com. Requests will be handled within 30 days.

External Services

  • Google Fonts · fonts loaded from Google's servers. Google may collect basic request data. Their privacy policy →

No other third-party services are used on this website.
