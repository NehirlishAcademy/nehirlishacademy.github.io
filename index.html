About

I teach English the Nehir way.

I've been teaching English since I was 16, and the years since have turned tutoring from something I did on the side into the thing I do best. I'm bilingual, fully fluent in both English and Turkish, so I know exactly where Turkish speakers get stuck, because I've stood in the same place myself. That's why I can explain things in a way that actually clicks, instead of the stiff textbook way.

My 1-on-1 lessons are built entirely around you: your goals, your pace, and the things you actually find hard. Never a fixed script. I keep it real, not academic: the only exam I prep is PTE (where I scored a perfect 90 in Speaking and Listening myself), and I make SAT tactics as friendly and un-scary as I possibly can. It all points to one thing: speaking with real confidence, not memorising rules.

My favourite students are the ones who arrive convinced they "can't." Watching someone go from that to actually speaking is the whole reason I do this. And the name? Simple: Nehir + English = Nehirlish. I teach English the Nehir way.
